    Aqualifting is a low-traumatic, absolutely effective and safe opportunity to make a facelift using small water jets. This method of restoring contours and getting rid of wrinkles was developed by Dr. Thomas Tork.     Any contraindications?

    Despite a fairly mild effect, Aqualifting has a number of contraindications:

    • Psychological disorders;
    • Neuroses;
    • Diabetes mellitus;
    • Pathology of cardiac muscle and vessels of acute or chronic nature;
    • Low blood coagulability;
    • Reduced haemoglobin level;
    • Acute infections of a bacterial or viral nature;
    • Neoplasms both malignant and benign;
    • Pregnancy and lactation;
    • Age under 20 years;
    • Age after 50 years.
