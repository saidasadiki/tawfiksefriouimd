    WHAT IS VASER 4D HI-DEF LIPOSUCTION?

    VASER 4D Hi-Def Liposuction is one of the newest variations of surgical fat removal treatment that offers more precise and controlled results and a much faster recovery time. VASER is a technological device that liquefies fat prior to extraction in order to ensure a smooth and gentle removal process. This greatly improves recovery times and reduces potential risks compared to traditional liposuction; which requires the relatively forceful removal of fat using a cannula that can also additional bruising and swelling in the treatment area. Aside from a more rapid recovery, the smoother extraction of fat improves results, leaving more even and effective results.

    VASER 4D Hi-Def Liposuction is not the ideal surgery for losing a lot of weight or undergoing drastic body transformations… It’s perfect for individuals that are close to their ideal weight with little body fat.     HOW DOES VASER 4D HI-DEF LIPO WORK?

    Dr. Sefrioui, through a series of small and inconspicuously placed incisions, will introduce a dilute anesthetic solution, as local anesthesia, into the area(s) being treated. Then the VASER 4D Hi-Def Liposuction cannula is presented through the same small discreet openings to melt and liquefy the unwanted fat. Lastly, small tubes, or “cannulas”, are placed through these same slits to gently aspirate and remove the liquefied unwanted fat.
